The native IPTV player
that plays everything.

Live TV, Movies, and Series in one fast desktop app. Built on mpv/libmpv, so it handles every container and codec — MKV, HEVC, EAC3, redirecting live HLS — that browser players choke on. Supports Xtream Codes and plain M3U playlists.

Everything you need to watch

🎬 Plays everything

Rendered through mpv/libmpv — embedded right in the window on macOS. Every codec, hardware decoding, rock-solid live streams.

📺 Live · Movies · Series

Browse thousands of channels, a full VOD library, and shows with season/episode pickers and auto-play next.

🔌 Xtream & M3U

Auto-detects Xtream Codes URLs, or load any M3U/M3U-plus file or link — classified into Live, Movies, and Series automatically.

🗣️ Audio & subtitles

Switch audio and subtitle tracks from a clean picker. Your choice is remembered per movie and across a whole series.

⭐ Favorites & resume

Star anything, pick up movies and episodes right where you left off, and see your progress at a glance.

⚡ Fast & auto-updating

Lazy-loaded lists handle huge playlists smoothly, and the app updates itself over the air from GitHub Releases.
